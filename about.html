Work About

I use collage to explore how systems shape behaviour — from digital interfaces to everyday rituals.

My work focuses on scale, control, and the small human actions that sit inside larger structures. It draws on visual culture, technology, and social systems to create images that are simple on the surface but open to interpretation.

Alongside this, I work as a presentation designer, helping organisations clarify complex ideas through visual structure and narrative.
