I use collage to explore how systems shape behaviour.

My work looks at digital interfaces, everyday processes, and the structures behind them.

Traditional collage uses printed material. I use fragments of digital systems instead. These include UI elements, system messages, and loading states.

I am interested in control, scale, and small human actions inside larger systems.

The images are simple on the surface, but they point to wider patterns.

Alongside this, I work as a graphic designer. I help organisations explain complex ideas through clear visual structure.
